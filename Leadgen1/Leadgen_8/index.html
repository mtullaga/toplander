Over A Million Seniors Have Taken Out A Reverse Mortgage For A Cash Infusion! Are You Next?


If you're like most seniors, you unfortunately don't have enough funds to comfortably retire. This is why millions of seniors are taking advantage of a reverse mortgage loan to help supplement their retirement income.

Why Should You Consider A Reverse Mortgage?

A reverse mortgage can be a great way to get a source of tax-free funds, while helping you get rid of your existing mortgage payment and generally, only having to pay for property taxes, maintenance and homeowner’s insurance.

You can commonly receive 50% of the equity in your home as a lump sum or through scheduled payments. You can keep the title of your home, stop paying monthly mortgage bills and receive enough cash to consider retirement.

It is crucial that you can go into your golden years with certainty, confidence and control. With this extra supplemental income, you can pay off overdue bills, repair or remodel your home, build a financial safety net, cover in-home medical costs, or even take the trip you've been wanting to go on.

Is It For You? Here Are The Benefits:

➡️ Live in your home and retain title as long as you continue to pay your property taxes, insurance, and maintenance.

➡️ Loan proceeds are commonly tax-free and you can use them any way you see fit.

➡️ No more monthly mortgage payments during the course of the loan.

➡️ Receive the funds how you want: one-time lump sum, line of credit, or a steady stream of monthly advances.

➡️ Generally, a reverse mortgage loan will not affect Social Security or Medicare benefits.

➡️ If your home value increases, you can always consider refinancing your reverse mortgage to access even more loan proceeds.
